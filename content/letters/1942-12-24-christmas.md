>

## Context

Christmas Eve 1942. Despite earning only 17 marks per month, Raisa has somehow saved and sent **50 marks** plus two parcels to her family in occupied Kyiv. She takes time to explain plowing to her young nephew Grishunok — describing the process with horses and cows in detail, as if teaching a child about a world he's never seen.

The contrast is stark: an 18-year-old forced laborer spending Christmas far from home, yet her thoughts are entirely with her family.
